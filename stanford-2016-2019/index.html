Stanford Years (2016 -- 2019)

  • Paper release: our new paper Dual-Reference Design for Holographic Coherent Diffraction Imaging is on arXiv. We show that augmenting both the pinhole and block references in holographic CDI can combine the best parts of both! (Feb 07, 2019)
  • Paper release: full version of the paper Holographic Phase Retrieval and Optimal Reference Design on arXiv. We show how the difficult Fourier phase retrieval problem can be substantially simplified and robustly solved in practical CDI (i.e., Coherent Diffraction Imaging) setting, with a simple augmented reference. (Jan 22, 2019)
